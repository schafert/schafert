abstract = "Selection of den sites is a crucial aspect of American black bear               (\textit{Ursus americanus}) life history. High-quality dens provide               thermal insulation, protection from disturbance, suitable environment               for parturition and cub development, and proximity to available forage               upon emergence. Slope was the best predictor of den site selection               and there was no relationship between den selection and housing               density, indicating that black bears were neither avoiding nor seeking               urban areas for denning."
